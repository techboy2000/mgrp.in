Rules You Can't Refuse

Our server has implemented some rules for the well being of roleplay. So it is strictly considered to follow these rules.

Discord Rules

- Toxicity, bullying and/or having offensive avatars of any kind is strictly against the rules and may result in a permanent ban
- Trolling or being an annoyance to anybody on Discord is not tolerated.
- Do not message any admin or anybody to review their application.
- You are never allowed to advertise any other FIVEM RP communities.
- Do not advertise any other Discord server within our community
- Trying to sell a physical product of yours on our Discord is also bannable.
- Disrespecting Panel Members are not allowed.
- Do not spam

Community Rules

- Bullying, Harassment, or Targeting is punishable and result in ban !
- Any form of discrimination, racism or sexism is punishable and result in ban !
- Rape Roleplay or vulgar language/actions are not tolerated !
- Swearing is not at all allowed even when you are in a roleplay !
- Any threats made to the community or a person will be taken very seriously !
- Encouraging or forcing other members of the community to break rules is strictly prohibited and will be punished with a ban !
- Providing false information such as your age when applying for a position/job is punishable!

In-Game Rules

- Impersonating any government official (Police/EMS) is not at all allowed. Players will be banned without warnings !
- Police have an Ingame Criminal Record ! All Warrents issued by the police is valid for 7 days , since MGRP follows 7 day RP !
- Use Voice changer in role play is not at all tolerated ! If found will result in serious actions !
- Speaking "OOC" through in game will result in spot kick from game !
- In MGRP city "Hospital" ,"Main-Garage" ,"Police station" & Community service area is considered as safe zone ! No one is allowed to use weapon or Fight or do any Illegal activity there!
